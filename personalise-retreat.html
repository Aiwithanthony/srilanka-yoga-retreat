OCEAN & WILD EXPERIENCES

Because Sri Lanka is meant to be experienced beyond the retreat. Discover some of the island’s most iconic wildlife, ocean and cultural experiences. Each excursion has been carefully selected and can be added to your journey based on availability. All prices are per person and include applicable service charges and taxes.

Surf the Indian Ocean

Catch your first wave with a beginner-friendly surf lesson on the southern coast at Anantara Peace Haven. Includes professional instructor, surfboard, rash guard, sunscreen & towel.
Approx. 1.5 hrs · Beginner friendly · Weather & sea conditions dependent

USD 145

Whale Watching

Head into the Indian Ocean in search of blue whales, sperm whales and dolphins. Includes boat excursion, light breakfast, life jacket, seasickness tablets, insurance & transfers.
Approx. 3–4 hrs · Early morning departure · Suitable for all levels

USD 90

Yala National Park Safari *

Private safari jeep through Sri Lanka’s most celebrated reserve — elephants, crocodiles, buffalo, birdlife and the elusive leopard. Includes jeep, transfers, driver & tracker and park fees.
Approx. 4–5 hrs · Wildlife sightings cannot be guaranteed

USD 150

Minneriya National Park Safari *

Witness elephants roaming Minneriya — during the seasonal “Gathering,” dozens congregate around the reservoir. Includes safari jeep, transfers, guide & park entrance.
Approx. 3–4 hrs · Seasonal · Weather dependent

USD 125

Tea Plucking Experience *

Join local pickers in the fields, tour a working tea factory and enjoy a guided Ceylon tea tasting. Includes plantation visit, transfers, plucking experience & tasting.
Approx. 1.5–2 hrs · Easy

USD 65

Sunrise Sigiriya Climb *

Climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress at sunrise, before the crowds, for golden light and panoramic views of this UNESCO site. Includes entrance ticket & local guide.
Approx. 2–3 hrs · Moderate (1,200+ steps)

USD 55

* Seasonal / subject to availability. Weather and wildlife conditions may affect the experience. Pre-booked retreat rates apply exclusively to experiences arranged before arrival.
