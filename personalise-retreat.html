OCEAN & ISLAND EXPERIENCES

Because Sri Lanka waters are meant to be lived!

Surf the Indian Ocean

Experience the thrill of riding your first wave with a beginner-friendly surf lesson. Includes surfboards, sunscreen, rashguards, towels, water & expert instruction.
1.5 hrs — ideal for first-timers

USD 105

Whale Watching

Set out on the Indian Ocean in search of blue whales, sperm whales and playful dolphin pods along Sri Lanka’s southern coast — one of the best whale-watching regions on earth.
Approx. 3–4 hrs — early morning departure, seasonal

Upon Request

Yala Jungle Safari

Experience one of Sri Lanka’s most iconic wildlife reserves on a guided safari through Yala National Park. Keep an eye out for elephants, crocodiles, sloth bears, exotic birdlife, and, with a little luck, the elusive Sri Lankan leopard.
Approx. 3–4 hrs — best at sunrise or late afternoon · Wildlife sightings cannot be guaranteed

Upon Request

Minneriya National Park — Wild Elephants

Witness one of Asia’s most remarkable wildlife experiences as herds of wild elephants roam freely through Minneriya National Park. Depending on the season, you may experience the famous “Gathering,” where dozens of elephants congregate around the reservoir.
Approx. 3 hrs — seasonal experience · Sightings depend on season and weather

Upon Request

Tea Plucking Experience

Step into Sri Lanka’s tea country and learn the traditional art of tea plucking alongside local tea pickers. Discover the journey from leaf to cup before enjoying a freshly brewed Ceylon tea overlooking the plantations.
Approx. 1 hr — authentic cultural experience

Upon Request

Sunrise Sigiriya Climb

Climb the legendary Sigiriya Rock Fortress at sunrise, when cooler temperatures and golden morning light reveal breathtaking panoramic views. Explore the ancient palace ruins and discover one of Sri Lanka’s most celebrated UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
Approx. 2–3 hrs — moderate climb (1,200+ steps) · Entrance fees included

Upon Request
